Due-Diligence Checklist (Before You Hire Any Roofer)

  • Request a written, itemized scope showing materials, square footage, and specific edges/flashing to be addressed.
  • Ask for before/after photos and confirm how long the warranty lasts and what area it covers.
  • Clarify debris and disposal charges and whether they are included or separate line items.
  • Confirm insurance and who is actually on the roof (owner vs. crew).
  • For full replacements, request a permit plan and brand/model of materials, not just “roofing.”
